Homemade Pancake Recipe

Simple, quick, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 250g flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 500ml milk
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour and salt.
  2. Add the eggs and whisk until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the milk while stirring.
  4. Add the oil and mix well.
  5. Let the batter rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Cook the pancakes in a lightly oiled hot pan.
